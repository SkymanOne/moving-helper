## 2. What Moving Buddy Does

Moving Buddy is a tool for tracking moving boxes by scanning barcode labels and updating their status in your Notion workspace. The application connects to Notion via OAuth and reads from and writes to only the databases you explicitly authorize.

## 3. Your Notion Data

Moving Buddy accesses your Notion workspace only with your explicit permission, and reads from or writes to only the databases you authorize. Your connection token is encrypted and stored on Cloudflare's edge network, associated with your workspace, and is never stored in your browser. If you generate a share code, the code is only a reference that allows invited guests to scan using your connection; it does not contain your token. You may revoke individual share codes, or disconnect your workspace entirely, at any time.
